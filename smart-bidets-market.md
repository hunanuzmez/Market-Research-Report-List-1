<p><p>The smart bidet market is categorized into two main types: one-piece and split-type smart bidets. One-piece smart bidets feature an integrated design where the bidet and toilet are combined, offering a sleek appearance and easier cleaning. In contrast, split-type smart bidets consist of a separate bidet seat attached to a traditional toilet, providing flexibility and typically a more affordable option. Both types enhance personal hygiene through advanced features such as heated seats, adjustable water pressure, and built-in dryers.</p></p>
