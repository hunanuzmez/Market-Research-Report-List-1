<p><ul><li>Whole-Home</li><li>Split Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air conditioning maintenance market can be categorized into two main types: whole-home systems and split systems. Whole-home systems are typically centralized units that cool air throughout an entire house, requiring regular maintenance to ensure optimal performance and energy efficiency. In contrast, split systems consist of an indoor unit and an outdoor compressor, allowing for controlled cooling in specific areas. Each type demands distinct maintenance approaches, including routine inspections, cleaning of coils, and refrigerant checks to sustain effectiveness and longevity.</p></p>
