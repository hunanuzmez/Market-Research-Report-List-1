<p><p>The semi-fermented tea market is categorized into three types based on oxidation levels. Medium semi-fermented teas, oxidized between 20% and 50%, present a richer taste, attracting a broader audience. Heavy semi-fermented teas, ranging from 50% to 80% oxidation, deliver bold, robust flavors, often favored by experienced tea drinkers seeking depth and complexity in their cup.</p></p>
