<p><p>In-line water heaters are categorized into ground mount and wall mount types based on installation preferences and space availability. Ground mount heaters are typically placed on the floor and are well-suited for larger spaces, offering ease of access for maintenance. In contrast, wall mount heaters are installed on walls, saving floor space and making them ideal for smaller areas. Both types provide efficient water heating solutions, catering to residential and commercial needs while optimizing energy usage and convenience.</p></p>
