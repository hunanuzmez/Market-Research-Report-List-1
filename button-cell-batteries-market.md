<p><p>The button cell battery market comprises several types, each suited for specific applications. Silver oxide batteries (SR) offer stable voltage and are commonly used in watches and calculators. Alkaline button cell batteries (LR) are cost-effective with moderate energy output, suitable for low-drain devices. Zinc-air batteries are primarily used in hearing aids due to their efficient energy conversion. Other types may include specialty batteries for niche applications.</p></p>
