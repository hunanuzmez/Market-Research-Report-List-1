<p><ul><li>Liquid based</li><li>Non-liquid tape</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optically clear adhesive tape market can be divided into two primary types: liquid-based and non-liquid tape. Liquid-based adhesive tapes utilize a wet adhesive that requires application and curing, providing strong bonding characteristics while allowing for flexibility in use. In contrast, non-liquid tapes come pre-coated with adhesive, offering immediate adhesion and ease of application. Both types are essential for industries like electronics and glass, ensuring high clarity, durability, and efficient performance in various applications.</p></p>
