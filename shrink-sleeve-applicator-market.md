<p><p>The Shrink Sleeve Applicator market is segmented into semi-automatic and fully automatic types. Conversely, fully automatic applicators streamline the entire packaging process, integrating product feeding, wrapping, and heat shrinking, which increases efficiency and reduces labor costs. Both types cater to various industries, ensuring effective packaging solutions for diverse product lines.</p></p>
