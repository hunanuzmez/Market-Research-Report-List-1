<p><strong>The Thyroid Home Test Kits Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>TSH Tests</li><li>FT4 Tests</li><li>FT3 Tests</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thyroid home test kits typically include TSH, FT4, and FT3 tests, each serving a distinct purpose in assessing thyroid function. TSH (Thyroid Stimulating Hormone) tests measure the pituitary gland's stimulation of the thyroid, indicating potential hypothyroidism or hyperthyroidism. FT4 (Free Thyroxine) tests assess the level of thyroxine hormone, critical for metabolism and energy regulation. FT3 (Free Triiodothyronine) tests evaluate the active form of thyroid hormone, which influences various bodily functions. Together, these tests provide a comprehensive view of thyroid health.</p></p>
