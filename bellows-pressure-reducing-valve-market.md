<p><ul><li>Positive</li><li>Counteractive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Bellows Pressure Reducing Valve market can be categorized into two main types: positive and counteractive. Positive pressure reducing valves maintain a constant downstream pressure by generating a force that counteracts upstream pressure fluctuations. In contrast, counteractive valves adjust flow based on varying downstream demands, responding dynamically to changes in pressure. Both types serve crucial roles in fluid control systems, ensuring efficient operation and safety in various industrial applications, contributing to optimized performance and energy savings.</p></p>
