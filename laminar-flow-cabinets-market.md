<p><strong>The Laminar Flow Cabinets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Horizontal Laminar Flow Cabinets</li><li>Vertical Laminar Flow Cabinets</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Laminar flow cabinets are essential in laboratory settings for maintaining a sterile working environment. Horizontal laminar flow cabinets direct air horizontally across the workspace, protecting samples from contamination while allowing easy access for the user. In contrast, vertical laminar flow cabinets provide downward airflow, creating a sterile zone above the work surface, ideal for applications requiring high levels of protection for both the user and the product. Both types cater to different lab requirements, ensuring safety and cleanliness in various procedures.</p></p>
