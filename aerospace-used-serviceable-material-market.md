Component materials include various aircraft system parts, such as landing gear and avionics, which can be overhauled. Body materials refer to the structural elements of aircraft, like fuselage sections, that are salvaged and repurposed, ensuring cost-effectiveness and sustainability within the aerospace industry.</p></p>
