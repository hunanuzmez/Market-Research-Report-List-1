<p><p>The Armored Unmanned Underwater Vehicle market comprises two primary types: Remotely Operated Vehicles (ROVs) and Autonomous Underwater Vehicles (AUVs). ROVs are tethered vehicles operated by human pilots from a surface platform, ideal for tasks requiring real-time control, like inspection and maintenance. In contrast, AUVs operate independently using pre-programmed missions, suitable for applications including oceanographic research and environmental monitoring. Both types are increasingly armored to withstand harsh underwater conditions, enhancing their operational capabilities and durability in various marine environments.</p></p>
