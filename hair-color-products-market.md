<p><strong>The Hair Color Products Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Temporary Hair Dye</li><li>Semi-permanent Hair Dye</li><li>Permanent Hair Dye</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hair color products market is categorized into three main types: temporary, semi-permanent, and permanent hair dyes. Temporary hair dyes coat the hair shaft and typically wash out after a few shampoos, making them ideal for short-term color changes. Semi-permanent dyes provide longer-lasting color, usually lasting several weeks and gradually fading without a harsh regrowth. Permanent hair dyes penetrate the hair shaft, offering lasting color that endures until the hair grows out or is cut, making them popular for a more committed color change.</p></p>
