<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Aircraft Engine Gearbox Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reduction Gearbox (RGB)</li><li>Accessory Gearbox (AGB)</li><li>Actuation Gearbox</li><li>Tail Rotor Gearbox</li><li>APU-Gearbox</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aircraft engine gearbox market comprises several types, each serving distinct functions. The Reduction Gearbox (RGB) reduces engine speed for optimal propeller or rotor performance. The Accessory Gearbox (AGB) drives engine accessories like generators and fuel pumps. Actuation Gearboxes facilitate control of flight surfaces. Tail Rotor Gearboxes transmit power to the tail rotor in helicopters. APU Gearboxes connect to Auxiliary Power Units for additional power. Other gearboxes cater to specialized applications, enhancing the overall functionality and efficiency of aircraft engines.</p></p>
