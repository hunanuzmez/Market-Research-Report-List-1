<p><p>Demineralization systems are essential for purifying water by removing mineral ions. The market is categorized into two types: semi-automatic and automatic systems. Semi-automatic systems require manual intervention for operations such as regeneration and monitoring, making them cost-effective and suitable for smaller applications. In contrast, automatic systems operate independently, utilizing advanced technology for real-time monitoring and automated regeneration processes, which enhances efficiency and reduces labor costs. This makes automatic systems ideal for larger industries with high water demand.</p></p>
