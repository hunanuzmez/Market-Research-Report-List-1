<p><p>The window regulator market is primarily divided into two types: electric and manual window regulators. Electric window regulators utilize motorized mechanisms to allow users to control window movement with a switch, offering convenience and ease of use. They are popular in modern vehicles due to their added features, such as one-touch operation. In contrast, manual window regulators rely on a crank handle for operation, providing a more traditional and cost-effective solution. Their simplicity makes them appealing for budget vehicles and older models.</p></p>
