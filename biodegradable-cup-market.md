<p><p>The biodegradable cup market primarily consists of two types: paper-coated products and thermoforming products. Paper-coated cups are made from paper substrates lined with biodegradable coatings, providing a sustainable option for beverages while maintaining structural integrity. On the other hand, thermoforming products are produced by shaping biodegradable plastics through heat and pressure, offering designs suitable for various applications. Both types prioritize environmental responsibility, catering to eco-conscious consumers and businesses seeking to reduce their carbon footprint.</p></p>
