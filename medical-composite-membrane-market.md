<p><p>The medical composite membrane market comprises various types, including PSU (Polysulfone) and PESU (Polyether Sulfone), known for their excellent thermal stability and biocompatibility. PVDF (Polyvinylidene Fluoride) offers high chemical resistance, while PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene) is valued for its non-stick properties and durability. PP (Polypropylene) is lightweight and cost-effective, whereas modified acrylics enhance adhesion and flexibility. Collectively, these materials cater to diverse medical applications such as filtration, wound dressing, and drug delivery systems, ensuring optimal performance in healthcare settings.</p></p>
