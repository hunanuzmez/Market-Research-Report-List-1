<p><ul><li>Light Stabilizers</li><li>Processing Stabilizers</li><li>Antioxidants</li><li>Sulfur-containing Co-stabilizers,</li><li>Metal Deactivators</li><li>Antistatic Agents</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polymer additives market includes various types of additives that enhance polymer performance. Light stabilizers protect polymers from UV degradation, extending their lifespan. Processing stabilizers improve polymer stability during manufacturing, preventing thermal degradation. Antioxidants inhibit oxidation, maintaining polymer integrity. Sulfur-containing co-stabilizers work synergistically with other additives to enhance thermal stability. Metal deactivators prevent metal-induced degradation in polymers. Antistatic agents reduce static electricity buildup, improving safety and usability. These additives collectively ensure polymers meet performance, durability, and safety requirements across industries like packaging, automotive, and construction.</p></p>
