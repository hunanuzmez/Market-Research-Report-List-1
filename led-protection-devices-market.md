<p><p>The LED protection devices market encompasses several key types that safeguard LED lighting systems. Shunt protection devices redirect excess current during faults, preventing damage to the LED fixture. Surge protective devices mitigate voltage spikes from surges, enhancing longevity and performance. Bypass protection devices allow current to bypass malfunctioning components, ensuring continued operation while isolating faults. Other market segments may include thermal protection devices and overvoltage protection that further enhance the reliability and efficiency of LED systems across various applications.</p></p>
