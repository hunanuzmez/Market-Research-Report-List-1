Optically Variable Ink (OVI) features colors that shift in different lighting, providing visual security. Magnetic Ink Character Recognition (MICR) enables the secure processing of checks by using magnetic ink, ensuring accurate reading by machines. Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) combines security and tracking by embedding chips in printed materials, allowing real-time data access and monitoring, further reinforcing the integrity of printed items.</p></p>
