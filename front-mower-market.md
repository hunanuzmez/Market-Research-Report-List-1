<p><p>The front mower market is categorized into three primary types: ride-on lawn mowers, walk-behind mowers, and self-propelled mowers. Ride-on lawn mowers are designed for larger lawns, offering comfort and efficiency with operators seated. Walk-behind mowers require manual propulsion, suitable for smaller areas with more maneuverability. Self-propelled mowers combine the features of push mowers with powered propulsion, making them user-friendly for varying terrain. Each type caters to different yard sizes and user preferences, enhancing mowing efficiency and convenience.</p></p>
