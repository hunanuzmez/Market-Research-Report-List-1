<p><p>The biocatalysts market is segmented into four main types: oxidoreductases, transferases, hydrolases, and others. Oxidoreductases facilitate oxidation-reduction reactions, essential in metabolic processes. Transferases transfer functional groups between molecules, playing a crucial role in synthesizing complex compounds. Hydrolases catalyze hydrolysis reactions, breaking bonds through the addition of water, vital in digestion and biodegradation. "Others" encompasses various lesser-known enzymes and biocatalytic processes, reflecting the diverse applications in pharmaceuticals, food production, and environmental management, driving market growth.</p></p>
