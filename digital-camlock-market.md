<p><p>The digital camlock market can be categorized into two main types: passive and active. Passive markets involve locks that operate autonomously without real-time management, typically using pre-set codes or biometric features. They offer ease of use and quick access but have limited monitoring capabilities. On the other hand, active markets involve smart locks integrated with IoT technology, enabling real-time control, surveillance, and remote access management. This type offers enhanced security features, making it suitable for high-security needs.</p></p>
