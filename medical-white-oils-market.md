<p><p>The Medical White Oils market is primarily categorized into paraffin-based and cycloalkyl-based types. Paraffin-based oils are derived from hydrocarbons and are widely used for their excellent purity and stability, making them ideal for pharmaceuticals and cosmetic applications. Cycloalkyl-based oils, on the other hand, feature a cyclic structure, offering enhanced performance in specific formulations. Both types are valued for their non-toxic properties and compatibility with various medical and cosmetic products, ensuring safe use in diverse applications.</p></p>
