<p><p>Pick-and-place handlers are automated systems used in various industries to efficiently move and position items on production lines. <p><p>Pick-and-place handlers are robotic systems used in automation for positioning components in manufacturing and assembly processes. The market can be categorized into three types based on the number of operational sites: Single Site handlers operate at one location, optimizing precision for specific tasks. Dual Site handlers allow simultaneous operations at two locations, increasing efficiency. Four Site handlers maximize productivity by functioning at four distinct sites, catering to high-throughput environments. Each type enhances workflow based on the complexity and scale of operations.</p></p>
