<p><p>The antifriction bearings market is primarily segmented into ball bearings and roller bearings. Ball bearings utilize spherical balls to reduce friction between moving parts, offering high-speed performance and load distribution, ideal for applications like motors and machinery. Roller bearings use cylindrical rollers, providing enhanced load-carrying capacity and stability, making them suitable for heavy-load applications like automotive and industrial equipment. Both types are essential for improving efficiency and longevity in mechanical systems by minimizing wear and heat generation.</p></p>
