<p><strong>The Time-of-flight (ToF) Chip Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Indirect</li><li>Direct</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Time-of-Flight (ToF) chip market is primarily categorized into indirect and direct types. Indirect ToF chips measure distance by analyzing the phase difference of light reflected from an object over time, making them suitable for applications requiring precise depth data, like 3D cameras. Direct ToF chips, on the other hand, calculate distance by emitting short light pulses and measuring the time it takes for them to return, offering faster and more straightforward depth estimation, ideal for gesture recognition and automotive applications.</p></p>
