<p><p>The Smart Game Phone market is characterized by two primary cooling technologies: air cooling and water cooling. Air cooling utilizes fans and heat sinks to dissipate heat generated during gameplay, making it cost-effective and widely adopted. In contrast, water cooling systems employ liquid to absorb and transfer heat away from critical components, offering superior thermal management and performance for high-end gaming phones. Both methods aim to enhance user experience by maintaining optimal performance during extended gaming sessions.</p></p>
