<p><p>The soaker hose market primarily features two types: flat soaker hoses and round soaker hoses. Flat soaker hoses are designed to lie flat on the ground, providing even water distribution over a wide area, making them ideal for gardens or flower beds. In contrast, round soaker hoses are circular in shape, facilitating targeted watering in vegetable patches or rows. Both types are efficient in delivering moisture directly to plant roots, reducing evaporation and promoting healthier plant growth while conserving water.</p></p>
