<p><strong>The Polyurethane Laminating Adhesive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent Based Laminating Adhesives</li><li>Solventless Laminating Adhesives</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyurethane laminating adhesive market is categorized into two primary types: solvent-based and solventless laminating adhesives. Solvent-based laminating adhesives utilize organic solvents in their formulation, providing strong adhesion and flexibility, suitable for various substrates. In contrast, solventless laminating adhesives, which contain no volatile organic compounds (VOCs), are environmentally friendly and offer quick curing times. Both types are widely used in packaging, automotive, and textile industries, offering excellent bonding properties and enhancing the durability of laminated products.</p></p>
