<p><strong>The Music Copyright Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Royalty Free (RF)</li><li>Rights Managed (RM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Music copyright markets primarily consist of Royalty Free (RF) and Rights Managed (RM) licensing. RF licenses allow users to pay a one-time fee for unlimited use of the music without ongoing royalties, making it cost-effective for projects with multiple applications. In contrast, RM licensing requires users to pay for specific usages, with fees varying based on factors like distribution and duration, which provides greater control for the rights holder while also catering to unique project needs.</p></p>
