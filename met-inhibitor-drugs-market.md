<p><p>The MET Inhibitor Drugs market comprises various types aimed at targeting the MET signaling pathway, which is often implicated in cancer. C-Met Biological Inhibitors involve larger molecules disrupting MET function, while Small Molecule C-Met Inhibitors are compact compounds that block MET activity. C-Met Antagonist Antibodies specifically target the MET protein to inhibit its function. HGF Antagonist Antibodies block the hepatocyte growth factor (HGF), preventing its interaction with MET. Kringle Variant Antagonists are designed to interfere with variations of HGF, providing additional therapeutic strategies.</p></p>
