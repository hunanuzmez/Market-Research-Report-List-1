<p><strong>The Terahertz Imaging Technology Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Terahertz Imaging Systems</li><li>Passive Terahertz Imaging Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Terahertz Imaging Technology Market is segmented into two main types: active and passive terahertz imaging systems. Active terahertz imaging systems generate their own terahertz radiation to capture images, providing enhanced control and optimized imaging quality, often used in industrial and security applications. In contrast, passive terahertz imaging systems detect naturally occurring terahertz radiation, making them suitable for applications such as medical diagnostics and non-destructive testing, where they leverage thermal emissions for imaging purposes.</p></p>
