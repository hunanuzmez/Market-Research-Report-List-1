<p><p>The curing adhesives market comprises three primary types: UV cure, moisture cure, and thermal cure adhesives. UV cure adhesives use ultraviolet light to initiate the curing process, providing rapid bonding and precise application. Moisture cure adhesives rely on the presence of moisture in the environment to activate the curing process, making them suitable for various conditions. Thermal cure adhesives require heat to facilitate curing, offering strong bonds in applications where elevated temperatures can be maintained. Each type serves distinct industry needs.</p></p>
