<p><strong>The Aircraft Thermal Management Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air Cycle</li><li>Vapor Cycle</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Aircraft Thermal Management Systems market is primarily segmented into Air Cycle and Vapor Cycle systems. Air Cycle systems utilize ambient air for cooling, employing a series of compressors and heat exchangers, making them lighter and efficient for high-altitude operations. Conversely, Vapor Cycle systems operate on a refrigeration cycle, using refrigerants to absorb and dissipate heat, providing effective thermal control in various operating environments. Both technologies play crucial roles in enhancing aircraft performance and passenger comfort, with distinct advantages for different applications.</p></p>
