<p><ul><li>Laser Scanner</li><li>Linear Imager Scanner</li><li>2D Imager Scanner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable barcode scanner market offers various types, including laser scanners, linear imager scanners, and 2D imager scanners. Laser scanners use laser beams for fast and accurate scanning, ideal for traditional 1D barcodes. Linear imager scanners capture images of barcodes using a single line of light, making them effective for scanning linear barcodes at a moderate speed. In contrast, 2D imager scanners can read both 1D and 2D barcodes, providing versatility and enhanced scanning capabilities for complex codes.</p></p>
