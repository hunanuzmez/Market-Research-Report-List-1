<p><p>Warehouse and logistic robots (WLR) encompass various types designed to automate tasks in storage and distribution. Mobile robots navigate autonomously, transporting goods within facilities. Cartesian robots operate on three linear axes for precision tasks, while collaborative robots (cobots) work alongside humans safely. Articulated robots, resembling a human arm, provide flexibility for complex movements. SCARA robots excel in assembly and packaging with their rigid structure, while parallel robots offer high-speed and precise motion, ideal for pick-and-place applications in warehouses.</p></p>
