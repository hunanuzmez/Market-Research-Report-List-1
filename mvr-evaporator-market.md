<p><ul><li>Multi-Effect Evaporation</li><li>Vapor Recompression</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MVR (Mechanical Vapor Recompression) evaporator market encompasses two primary types: Multi-Effect Evaporation and Vapor Recompression. Multi-Effect Evaporation utilizes multiple stages to increase efficiency by utilizing the vapor generated in one stage to heat the next, reducing energy consumption. On the other hand, Vapor Recompression involves mechanically compressing vapor to regenerate heat, allowing for a more compact design and lower operational costs. Both types are favored for their energy efficiency, making them ideal for various industrial applications.</p></p>
