<p><ul><li>POF Film</li><li>PVC Film</li><li>PP/BOPP Film</li><li>PE Film</li><li>PET Film</li><li>PLA Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Shrink films are categorized into several types based on materials and applications. POF (polyolefin) film is known for its clarity and stretchability, making it ideal for retail packaging. PVC (polyvinyl chloride) film offers durability and is commonly used for wrapping products. PP/BOPP (polypropylene/biaxially oriented polypropylene) film provides excellent moisture barrier properties. PE (polyethylene) film is versatile and cost-effective for various packaging needs. PET (polyethylene terephthalate) film is recognized for its high strength and resistance. PLA (polylactic acid) film is a biodegradable option, appealing for eco-friendly applications.</p></p>
