<p><p>The Real-Time Health Monitoring System market can be categorized into two main types: Bedside Health Monitoring Systems and Remote Health Monitoring Systems. Bedside Health Monitoring Systems are designed to continuously track patients' vital signs and health metrics in clinical settings, enabling immediate medical intervention when necessary. In contrast, Remote Health Monitoring Systems allow patients to be monitored outside traditional healthcare environments, using wearable devices and digital platforms to transmit health data in real time to healthcare providers, enhancing accessibility and personalized care.</p></p>
