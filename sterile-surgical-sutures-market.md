<p><p>The sterile surgical sutures market is categorized into two main types: sterile absorbable surgical yarns and sterile nonabsorbable surgical yarns. Sterile absorbable surgical yarns are designed to be gradually absorbed by the body over time, eliminating the need for removal and promoting healing. In contrast, sterile nonabsorbable surgical yarns are intended for long-term support and remain in the body, providing durability for various surgical procedures. Both types are essential in ensuring effective wound closure and reducing infection risk.</p></p>
