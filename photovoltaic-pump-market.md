<p><strong>The Photovoltaic Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DC Surface Suction</li><li>AC Submersible</li><li>DC Submersible</li><li>AC Floating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photovoltaic pump market encompasses various types based on power supply and usage. DC surface suction pumps operate on direct current and are suitable for shallow water sources, ideal for surface irrigation. AC submersible pumps, powered by alternating current, are designed for deep-water extraction, commonly used in larger agricultural operations. DC submersible pumps are efficient for remote applications where solar energy is prevalent, while AC floating pumps are used in water bodies, providing energy-efficient solutions for irrigation and aquaculture.</p></p>
