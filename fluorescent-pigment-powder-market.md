<p><p>The fluorescent pigment powder market is categorized into three main types: thermoset, thermoplastic, and other formulations like aqueous dispersions. Thermoset pigments are known for their durability and resistance to heat, making them ideal for long-lasting applications. Thermoplastic pigments, on the other hand, remain pliable when heated, allowing for easier processing and application. Other types, such as aqueous dispersions, provide environmentally-friendly options and are suitable for various surfaces, enhancing color vibrancy while minimizing toxicity and waste.</p></p>
