<p><p>The Nano Titanium Dioxide Powder market is primarily segmented into two types: Rutile and Anatase. Rutile type is characterized by its high stability, UV-blocking capabilities, and superior whitening properties, making it ideal for applications in cosmetics, coatings, and plastics. In contrast, Anatase type offers higher photocatalytic activity and is commonly used in environmental applications, such as air and water purification. The choice between these types depends on the specific application requirements, including performance, stability, and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
