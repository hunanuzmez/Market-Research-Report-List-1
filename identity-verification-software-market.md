<p><strong>The Identity Verification Software Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Static Verification</li><li>Dynamic Verification</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Identity verification software can be categorized into two primary types: static and dynamic verification. Static verification involves checking fixed data, such as government-issued IDs or documents, to confirm a person's identity at a specific moment. In contrast, dynamic verification assesses identity in real-time, often through biometric features like facial recognition or behavioral analysis during transactions. Both methods aim to enhance security, but they serve different purposes and offer varying levels of assurance based on the context of use.</p></p>
