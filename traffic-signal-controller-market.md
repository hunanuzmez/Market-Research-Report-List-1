<p><p>The traffic signal controller market includes several key types. Centralized Adaptive Traffic Signal Controllers dynamically adjust signals based on real-time traffic conditions to optimize flow. Fixed Time Traffic Signal Controllers operate on pre-set timing schedules, suitable for predictable traffic patterns. Actuated Control Traffic Signal Controllers use sensors to detect vehicle presence and adjust signal timing accordingly, improving efficiency at intersections. Each type addresses specific traffic management needs, enhancing road safety and reducing congestion.</p></p>
