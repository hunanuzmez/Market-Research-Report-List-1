<p><p>The automatic conveyor market encompasses various types designed for specific material handling needs. Belt conveyors transport bulk materials on a moving belt, while vibrating conveyors utilize vibration to move products along a predetermined path. Pneumatic conveyors use air pressure to transport materials through tubes. Horizontal motion conveyors facilitate smooth lateral movement, and screw conveyors use a helical screw to propel materials. Additionally, the "others" segment includes specialized systems tailored for unique applications across diverse industries.</p></p>
