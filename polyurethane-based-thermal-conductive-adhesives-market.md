<p><strong>The Polyurethane Based Thermal Conductive Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Isotropic</li><li>Anisotropic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyurethane-based thermal conductive adhesives market consists of two main types: isotropic and anisotropic. Isotropic adhesives exhibit uniform thermal conductivity in all directions, making them suitable for applications where consistent heat dissipation is required regardless of orientation. In contrast, anisotropic adhesives provide better thermal conductivity in specific directions, which is advantageous for targeted heat management in electronic components. Both types cater to diverse industries, including electronics, automotive, and aerospace, leveraging their unique properties for efficient thermal management solutions.</p></p>
