<p><strong>The Thermal Printhead Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thick Film Thermal Printhead</li><li>Thin Film Thermal Printhead</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermal printhead market features two main types: thick film thermal printheads and thin film thermal printheads. Thick film thermal printheads use a thicker layer of resistive material, offering durability and high-efficiency printing, making them suitable for industrial applications. In contrast, thin film thermal printheads utilize a refined layer of resistive material, providing higher resolution and faster printing speeds, ideal for applications like retail and label printing. Both types cater to diverse sectors, enhancing the versatility of thermal printing technology.</p></p>
