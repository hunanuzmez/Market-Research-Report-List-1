<p><strong>The Swept Frequency Capacitive Sensing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Capacitance</li><li>Projected Capacitance</li><li>Self-Capacitance</li><li>Mutual Capacitance</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The swept frequency capacitive sensing market includes four primary types: </p><p>1. **Surface Capacitance** focuses on the measurement of capacitance at the surface of objects, primarily used in touch screens.</p><p>2. **Projected Capacitance** allows for larger touch areas and increased sensitivity over a wider range, often found in interactive displays.</p><p>3. **Self-Capacitance** measures the capacitance of individual electrodes, ideal for simple touch applications.</p><p>4. **Mutual Capacitance** detects touch by calculating changes in capacitance between paired electrodes, enabling multi-touch capabilities and more complex interfaces.</p></p>
