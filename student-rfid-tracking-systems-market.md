<p><ul><li>Tags</li><li>Readers</li><li>Middleware</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Student RFID tracking systems consist of three main market types: tags, readers, and middleware. Tags are small, embedded devices attached to student IDs or uniforms, allowing real-time identification and tracking. Readers are devices placed at strategic points to scan and capture data from the tags, facilitating attendance monitoring and location tracking in educational institutions. Middleware serves as the software layer that processes and manages the data collected, integrating it into existing systems for reporting and analytics, enhancing operational efficiency.</p></p>
