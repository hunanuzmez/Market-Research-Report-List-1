<p><p>The Single Use Bioreactors (SUBs) market comprises various types, including wave-induced motion SUBs, stirred SUBs, and other configurations. Wave-induced motion SUBs utilize gentle wave dynamics to promote mixing and enhance cell growth, minimizing shear stress. Stirred SUBs, on the other hand, employ mechanical stirring mechanisms for enhanced aeration and mixing, making them suitable for larger-scale applications. Other types may include fixed-film and modular designs, catering to diverse bioprocessing needs, including cell culture, fermentation, and product formulation.</p></p>
