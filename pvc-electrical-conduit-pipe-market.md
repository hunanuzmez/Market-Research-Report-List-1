<p><p>The PVC electrical conduit pipe market is divided into two main types: rigid and flexible conduits. Rigid conduit pipes are solid, durable, and designed for fixed installations, providing excellent protection against environmental factors and physical damage. In contrast, flexible conduit pipes are more adaptable, allowing for easy installation in tight spaces and around obstacles. Both types serve to protect electrical wiring, ensuring safety and compliance with electrical codes while accommodating varying installation needs across residential, commercial, and industrial applications.</p></p>
