<p><strong>The Automotive Differential Gears Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hypoid Differential Gears</li><li>Spiral Differential Gears</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive differential gears market is characterized by various types, including hypoid and spiral differential gears. Hypoid differential gears feature an offset between the drive shaft and the axle, allowing for smoother operation and efficient torque transfer. They are commonly used in light trucks and luxury vehicles. Spiral differential gears, known for their helical teeth, offer quieter operation and improved load distribution, making them suitable for high-performance applications. Both types significantly enhance vehicle handling and power delivery, contributing to advancements in automotive engineering.</p></p>
