<p><p>Biologics fill and finish services encompass processes that prepare biologic drugs for distribution, focusing on two primary market types: liquid and lyophilized. The liquid market involves filling vials or syringes with ready-to-use biologics, preserving their stability and efficacy. In contrast, the lyophilized market entails freeze-drying biologics to enhance shelf life and stability, transforming them into a powder form that can be reconstituted before use. Both markets are essential for ensuring the safe and effective delivery of biologic therapies.</p></p>
