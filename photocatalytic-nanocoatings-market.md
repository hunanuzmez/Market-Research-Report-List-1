<p><ul><li>Water-Borne</li><li>Solvent-Borne</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photocatalytic nanocoatings market is primarily categorized into two types: water-borne and solvent-borne. Water-borne nanocoatings utilize water as a solvent, offering environmentally friendly options with lower volatile organic compounds (VOCs). They are ideal for outdoor applications due to their durability and ease of application. Solvent-borne nanocoatings, on the other hand, use organic solvents, providing enhanced performance characteristics like quicker drying times and better adhesion. Each type serves specific needs in industries such as construction, automotive, and consumer products.</p></p>
