<p><p>The aqueous printing ink market is categorized by printing technologies, primarily including flexography, gravure, and digital printing. Flexography utilizes flexible relief plates for high-speed printing on various substrates, ideal for packaging. Gravure employs intaglio cylinders for high-quality, continuous printing, commonly used in magazines and labels. Digital printing offers versatility and quick turnaround by directly transferring digital images onto substrates, accommodating short runs and customization. Each technology presents unique advantages, catering to diverse industry needs and applications in aqueous ink formulation.</p></p>
