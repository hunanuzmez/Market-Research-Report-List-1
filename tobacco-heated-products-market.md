<p><strong>The Tobacco Heated Products Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intermediate Heating</li><li>Ring Heating</li><li>Compound Heating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tobacco Heated Products (THPs) are categorized into three market types based on their heating methods. Intermediate Heating involves moderate temperatures to release tobacco flavors, providing a smoother experience. Ring Heating uses a circular mechanism to evenly heat tobacco, enhancing consistency in flavor and aroma. Compound Heating combines different heating techniques to optimize the release of nicotine and taste, allowing for a customizable experience. Each method caters to distinct consumer preferences, driving growth in the THP market.</p></p>
